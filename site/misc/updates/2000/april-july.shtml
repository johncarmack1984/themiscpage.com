July 25th - Carmack
  • The Miscellaneous Page has returned after a brief period of redesign. Now, the site is much easier to navigate and far easier on the eyes than the old green text on a purple background look. In fact, I recommend that you thoroughly browse the site and get a feel for how it works. We would love some input on the new look, and if you have any suggestions, we would love to hear them. But, to show you that we haven't been simply tweaking HTML for the past three weeks, I've got some nifty new stuff for you to look at. Here's the update today as far as content goes:
  • New midi: "AfterShock," by Carmack (me).
  • Inside the new Miscellaneous section, you can find a small chat application that uses Macromedia Shockwave. This could become useful.
  • Added another new section, Downloads. Give it a once over if you care to.
  • A new joke for you today! It's called Jesus in a Bar and was sent to me by mailbits.com. I heartily endorse this site, and recommend it to everyone here.
  • Inside the downloads section, you will find our first download: wassup.mov. A description of it is available inside the downloads section.
  • First staff member: Dixon has volunteered his services to The Miscellaneous Page as co-webmaster. His duties will begin whenever I can find a worthwhile assignment for him. He'll also be updating from time to time.
  • For all you folks who have ever wanted to work for a webpage, I'm in need of staff. For any interested, please visit www.themiscpage.com/staffcall.shtml by typing it in your browser or by following the preceding link.
  • Remember, this redesign is still not finished. I have yet to do the webring section and the main page completely. Any input you folks have would be appreciated. You can follow the link to an email form where it says "(last update by Carmack)."
  • And that concludes the biggest update in TMP history. Next update will be on Friday.
