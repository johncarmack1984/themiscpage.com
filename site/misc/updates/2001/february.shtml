February 2001


March 4th - Carmack
Well, Good evening to all of you! Thanks for coming by!
  • First off, I've got some really exciting news for you. Well, it may not be exciting to you, but personally, I'm just thrilled. For over a month now, I've had the melody to a song written up, and I was all ready to record it and everything, but my problem was that I had no words. I couldn't think of a single thing to say. But, after many long hours of sitting around staring at paper, inspiration struck. I got down a chorus and two verses, and thus I've got an almost complete original song. It's a Praise & Worship type song, so any Christians who come by here might be interested in it. But, don't feel like you've got to be a Christian to listen to it; I would be downright thrilled if this were to be a popular song someday. And it definitely wouldn't bother me if that popularity came from the visitors to this website. It's in the music section.
  • A friend has made a spiffy little graphic for the Pics & Art section, which I've decided to call "The Misc Page" (well what else would I call it?).
Three Sundays in a row. This may work after all.
- Carmack
February 25th - Carmack
Good day! Here I am, Sunday night, just like I said! Now, all you skeptics, just go home and leave us alone. Shoo! Shoo!
  • Some new moderators for the forums today. After starting the Board of Artistic Merit, I figured we would need some people to watch over the people like me who tend to run rampant with useless thought. That kind of thing has no merit on a Board of Artistic Merit. So, I have chosen Arctik Max, Justin, SingCal and ZeldaBoy to be our moderators for the board. And I've heard that they are accepting bribes, so get in line while it's still short.
  • A new Narrative Joke was added today. This one is called "Fashion Victim" and is probably one of my favorites.
  • A new addition to the Pics & Art section was made today. I added one of my own pieces, entitled "I am Not Alone." Enjoy.
And that, my friends, is why we never let a weasel conduct traffic.
- Carmack
February 18th - Carmack
Hey, folks. Did you miss me? I tell you, nothing slows a website down more than a lazy webmaster. But, I'm back on the job and hope to accomplish great things from now on.
  • First off, a bit of a news item. From now on, I plan to have TMP updated once a week, usually on Sunday. If not then, I'll specify the day. I think by having a set plan for doing things, I'll have an easier time making up a schedule. I also hope to have the other staff members do their parts and update every now and then, but I only plan to have them update once every two weeks or so. The main responsibility for the site will be on my shoulders. So, good things for the future.
  • But, for the main update, I've got a bit of good news for those of you who visit the forums: InfoPop's software has improved vastly, with their new version running much quicker and offering many more features. If you haven't already seen the new forums, I recommend you do so quickly.
  • Small music update: I was playing guitar a bit, recording a bit, singing a bit, and doing some other stuff a bit, and I ended up with a small MP3 of me singin' and playing the guitar. The song is called Blind Man, and it's of the Christian persuasion. If you really are interested in hearing what my singing voice sounds like, go for it. But, be warned: I fall flat on the third chorus... blech. Enjoy, and good listening.
  • A new sub-section has been added to the "Miscellaneous" part of the site: Miscellaneous Diction. For a description, I suggest you visit. It's all very much fun.
  • And you do the hokey pokey...
And that's what it's all about.
- Carmack
