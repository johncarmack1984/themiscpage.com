Miscellaneous Diction
Here at The Miscellaneous Page, we're very protective of our visitors. And, since we don't want anyone to be able to corrupt you with their modern day speech paterns. As such, we've created our own little vernacular, made of mixed words from the 70s and 80s, common phrases that nobody really uses, and other such tidbits. We'll add new stuff every now and then, just to keep you on your toes. But remember: use this only for good, never evil.
Word What We Want it to Mean
Faaaantastic adj. - The meaning of this one really isn't all that different from the traditional one: Good. Not bad. The opposite of unpleasant. The key is in the pronunciation. Listen to the sound file to hear what I speak of.
Example: That was one faaaantastic meal we just had.
Mondo adj. - Meaning "large" or "immense"; sizable
Example: That was one mondo meal we just had.
Cowabunga int. - A simpler way of saying "Gee, what I'm doing right now is very exciting or noteworthy!"
Example: I just won $1,000,000.00 on TV, cowabunga!
