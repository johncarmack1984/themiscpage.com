Herbie the Cat
This, folks, is Herbie the Cat. He is the pet/son of a good friend of mine. While he is only 3, he has mastered the art of instant sleep, and no matter what she does, she can't convince him to tell her his secret of slumber. About 15 pounds, I'd say, and I imagine that about 3/4 of that weight is his fur. Still, he's the cutest cat I know, and I knew he deserved a spot on the web. I give you folks... Herbie: Cat of Destiny. Hehe.
