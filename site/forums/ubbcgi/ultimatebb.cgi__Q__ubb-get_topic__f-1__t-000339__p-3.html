How many times do I have to say it? I made my decision to stop eating meat long before I heard of PETA.

"... it [meat] has vitamins and minerals I can't get anywhere else ..."

Not true, Zoop. Anything that we need can be gotten from a plant-based diet. And ulike meat, vegetables don't have extremely high quanities of bad thigns like fat and cholesterol.

According to the American Dietetic Association, "Vegan and vegetarian diets can significantly reduce one's risk of contracting heart disease, colon and lung cancer, osteoporosis, diabetes, kidney disease, hypertension, obesity, and a number of other debilitating conditions."

Eggs have more cholesterol than any other food, making them the leading cause of heart disease. Cows milk contains far too much fat and protein for humans (but is at just the right level for calves). Cows milk is no longer required or recommended.

Most vegan foods contain low levels of fat, and no cholesterol, so they are much healthier sources of fiber and nutrients than meat. Calcium, protein, iron, etc. can all be gotten from plant-based foods.

Do your homework next time.

Then on top of all of the health benifits, there's the cruelety side of it. Do you think battery-cages, castration of piglets and calves, chicken occasionaly being boiled alive (and sometimes even making it through the boiling alive!) are myths?
