I was in a convenience store today talking to a couple of my friends, and a female acquaintance who works in the store. At one point in the conversation, nobody was talking, and I made eye contact with the girl. She said to me, "Hey, John, how are you?"

My response (said in a somewhat joking manner): "Well, since you ask, I'm feeling a little bit down. I think I need some reassuring physical female contact."

To which she immediately responded, "Well, I could kiss you if you think it would make you feel better."

Being the kind of guy I am, I politely declined. But still, the offer did boost the ol' self-confidence ever so slightly. I'm happy, you're happy, everybody's happy!
