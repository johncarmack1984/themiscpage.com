The Top 10 Reasons why I love Christmas
    Christmas is my favorite holiday. It’s just so much fun. From the first Christmas shopping to the day I take the tree down, I love it. There’s so much to do during the Christmas season, I’m always busy with something. This is a list of my ten favorite things about the Christmas season.

10.) Putting the tree up. Who knew that something that’s this much work could also be so much fun. I love putting the tree up. Maybe because it gives me something to do besides sit around on the internet. Oh well, it’s still a lot of fun.

9.) Christmas dinner. Every Christmas Day, my family has a huge dinner with great food. It usually takes until next Christmas before I can move again because I ate so much. :-)

8.) Seeing Santa. Yes, It’s true. I’ve seen Santa. He was standing outside the store ringing a bell and asking for money in the name of the Salvation Army. Seeing him just makes my day.

7.) Eggnog. Yup, I’m one of the people who likes it. In fact, I’m about the only one I know who likes it. No one else does. I think it is great though. They should make it year round.

6.) Christmas movies. Christmas movies are some of the best things ever to be played on t.v.. There are lots of great ones during the Christmas season. But the best is “How the Grinch Stole Christmas”. I can’t see how anybody could NOT like this one. It’s the best.

5.) All the decorations. They’re everywhere, in the stores, in peoples yards, even on peoples roofs. All kinds of Santas and sleighs and reindeer. They’re so funny to see.

4.) Getting presents. Who doesn’t like this part? I love it. Getting the new N64 or computer game I wanted, or the new CD I was hoping for. This is one of the best parts of Christmas.

3.) Giving presents. As much as I like receiving them, giving presents out is always better to me. Seeing the joy on peoples face when they see what my hard-earned money bought for them is great. :-)

2.) Going out looking at Christmas lights. This is what I wait all year for. In fact, I’ve already gone looking at them. I went the day after Thanksgiving. Some people have huge displays up. It’s just so great to see them.

1.) And the number one thing I like about the Christmas season is, Christmas music! I think it’s the best music on the planet! On my computer, I listen to Christmas midis. When I’m not on the comp, I listen to CDs of it. I just can’t get enough of Christmas carols. THEY are the single best thing about the holiday season. :-)

There you go, my opinion on the best things of the Christmas season. It’s so hard to pick out my fav things about it. It’s also hard waiting for Christmas to come around. I wish it came more than once a year. Oh well, sometimes the waiting is the best part. I’ll see you all later! :-)

~Socerbal
