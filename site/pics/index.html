Pics & Art
TMP has a very talented group of visitors, and many of them enjoy art and graphics design. Below are some pictures and images that can only be classified as "Miscellaneous." Grouped by artist/photographer.

Circle Thing What happens when boredom strikes upon my weary soul. It's just two intertwining circles with a trippy background.

I am Not Alone While I was learning how to use Photoshop, one of my exercises was to create a 3-D button and add some glowing text. I needed a phrase, and there you have it.
