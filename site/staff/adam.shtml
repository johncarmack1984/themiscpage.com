staff - noun
a. a stick or cane carried as an aid in walking or climbing
b. a rule or similar graduated stick used for testing or measuring, as in surveying
c. the personnel who carry out a specific enterprise

Miscellaneous
  Adam Hughes
last updated June 25th, 2003
(contact Adam)

Under unfortunate circumstance I was born in the highly republican state of Texas.  Do not judge me on my residency, but on my constant struggle to survive there.  I consider myself an actor, writer, and artist all bundled in one.  From me you are allowed to expect something cynical yet uplifting, for in my perspective, life is funny – especially the depressing parts.   Such is the tale told from a liberal atheist in conservative surroundings.  Pity me if you must, send money if you can, and by all means, bask in any glory I may accomplish.  This has been a clever horse production, have a drink on me.
