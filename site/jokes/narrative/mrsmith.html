Mr. Smith
Mr. Smith is driving to work one morning. His conscience (the little voice in his head) speaks to him. "Mr. Smith, you're going to be late, AGAIN." Mr. Smith responds by saying, "Well, what can I do about it?"So they enter into the following dialogue:
Conscience: "Well, you could drive a little faster"
Mr. Smith: "Okay." (pushes harder on the gas pedal)
Conscience: "That's better. Now give it a little more...
Mr. Smith: (Pushes a little harder)
Conscience: "A little more..."
(Soon Mr. Smith is doing a hundred and twenty)
Conscience: "Hey Mr. Smith! Watch out for that cliffffffffffffffffffffff..."
SCREEEEEEECH! CRASHHHHHHHH!
The police, who have been tailing him, catch up and watch the wreckage burn. Later, when an autopsy reveals that Mr. Smith was not drunk or stoned at the time of the accident, the chief of police requests a background check. When he learns the probable cause of the accident, he groans and shakes his head, saying, "Will they ever learn? What kind of fool doesn't realize that...

It's better to be Mr. Smith who was late...

than the late Mr. Smith.
