Country Customs
One day, a French man, a Dutch man, and an American rode in different airplanes on the same day. The French man said, "I will throw down some grapes to stand for my country." So he threw down the grapes. The Dutch man said, "I will throw down some cheese to stand for my country." So he threw the cheese down. The American said, "I will throw down a bomb the stand for my country." So he threw down the bomb.

The next day, the French man walked by a crying young women about the age of fifteen. He asked her why she was crying, and she said, "Some grapes fell from the sky, and my dress was ruined for my date." Then the man walked away from the depressed teenager. The Dutch man walked by a frowning old women and asked if there was something wrong. She replied with, "I was making ice cream for my daughter's wedding, and some cheese fell from the sky and ruined it. The man walked on, reminding himself not to throw things from planes anymore. The American walked by young boy, laughing insanely. He asked, "Why are you laughing so much?" The boy replied with, "When my dad farted, the house blew up."
