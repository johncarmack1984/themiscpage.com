March 4th - Carmack
Well, Good evening to all of you! Thanks for coming by!
  • First off, I've got some really exciting news for you. Well, it may not be exciting to you, but personally, I'm just thrilled. For over a month now, I've had the melody to a song written up, and I was all ready to record it and everything, but my problem was that I had no words. I couldn't think of a single thing to say. But, after many long hours of sitting around staring at paper, inspiration struck. I got down a chorus and two verses, and thus I've got an almost complete original song. It's a Praise & Worship type song, so any Christians who come by here might be interested in it. But, don't feel like you've got to be a Christian to listen to it; I would be downright thrilled if this were to be a popular song someday. And it definitely wouldn't bother me if that popularity came from the visitors to this website. It's in the music section.
  • A friend has made a spiffy little graphic for the Pics & Art section, which I've decided to call "The Misc Page" (well what else would I call it?).
Three Sundays in a row. This may work after all.
- Carmack
